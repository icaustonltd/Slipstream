What do we do?

Slipstream Digital is a digital consultancy that helps its clients optimise their websites and apps by

  • increasing conversion rates
  • improving the customer journey, and
  • maximising the quality of their website code

What is digital optimisation?

In a nutshell, it's getting your website and app visitors to do what you want them to do. Sounds simple, but there are specific steps that need to be followed to achieve success.

How do we do it?

Lots of marketers just go with a hunch when it comes to making changes to their websites. We are different. By using proven optimisation strategies we help our clients scientifically test different functionality or content options on their web pages to find the best performing version. This in turn increases their conversion rates and, as a result, their profitability.

We use split testing (A/B and multivariate) to systematically improve a company's digital results.

But the testing is just the tip of the iceberg.

To be successful, a company needs to have an optimisation strategy in place, with embedded processes, structures and teams.

We can support you all the way on this journey.

Why Slipstream Digital?
